2023 Writer's Strike

Home About The Strike Related Information Further Reading

On May 2, 2023, the Writer's Guild of America went on strike, demanding fairer wages and business practices from the Alliance of Motion Pictures and Television Producers. This website is dedicated to gathering and displaying relevant information regarding the strike, as well as any adjacent information that may help contextualize the issue.

This website features video reports, video interviews, short summarizations, and links to articles that provide more detailed reporting on the issue. Please note that the summarzations are written by me, therefore any subjective ideas or opinions they may contain do not reflect those of the Writer's Guild or the Producer's Alliance.
